Pizza

Description

This is how you make Pizza

Ingredients

  • Sourdough
  • Basil leaves
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Flick of da wrist

Steps

  1. Kneed the dough
  2. Put oven on pizzahot (315 dgr celsius)
  3. Make dough into circle
  4. Add Tomato sauce, Mozzarella and Oil
  5. Put in oven for 5 minutes
  6. Take it out and top with Basil leaves + Salt
